Who We Are.

To encounter God face to face, to be a home where youth find their purpose, and raise up leaders who will be lights on their campus and to the world.

Moses met God face to face. We're here to encounter God in all his glory, love, and power and to experience him for ourselves.

A Home for Youth

We are a home and a family for youth to discover their God-given purpose in life. Clue: it's not found in the thousand narratives our world teaches us about who we are; it's found in our Maker's heart.
